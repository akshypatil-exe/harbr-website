How it Works Under the Hood

Suno AI functions by converting text prompts into audio spectrogram representation, which is then upscaled using custom diffusion models. The version 4 architecture processes audio tokens in high resolution, resulting in stereo imaging and dynamic ranges that match professional standard formats.
