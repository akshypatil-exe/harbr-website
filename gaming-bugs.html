Anti-Gravity Physics Anomalies

Another open-world simulator had players flying across the skybox when colliding with fence structures. A physics engine error miscalculated kinetic velocity transfer upon collision, resulting in players' characters accelerating into orbit at speed. Clips of flying players flooded streaming channels.

The Developer Struggles

As codebases exceed hundreds of gigabytes, automated tests often fail to catch edge-case bugs that only occur under massive player load. Game studios are increasingly leaning on hotfix patches and post-launch community reporting to identify issues, turning early adopters into voluntary beta testers.
